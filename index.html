About us

At Retro Quizz, we're on a mission to keep the retro geek spirit alive! We live and breathe neon lights, 8-bit games, and pixelated adventures.

From the glory days of arcade cabinets to the early days of the internet, we're all about the tech and video games that shaped the geek culture we love today.

We’re passionate about sharing our nostalgia for the good old days with fellow geeks and anyone who’s ready to rediscover the magic of technology's golden era. We’re here to bring those classic vibes back, one quiz at a time. So grab your controllers, dust off your old consoles, and let’s celebrate the geeks who paved the way!
